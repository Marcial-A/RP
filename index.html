Common questions

Everything you wanted to ask before a sales call

How long does it take to get set up? +
Most organizations are live in under two weeks. Your dedicated onboarding specialist will import your existing data, configure your forms, and walk you through workflows. Smaller programs can go live in days.
What does payment processing cost? +
Rates start as low as 1.5% per transaction depending on volume, card mix, and plan tier. You'll get a custom rate breakdown on your demo call — most customers pay less than they did with their previous processor.
Will it work with the tools we already use? +
Yes. Regpack ships with native QuickBooks, Salesforce, and HubSpot integrations, Stripe and Authorize.Net for payments, Calendly for scheduling, and a full REST API for everything else.
Is my data safe? +
Regpack is PCI-DSS Level 1 certified (the highest level), SOC 2 Type II audited, and GDPR-compliant. We never touch raw card data — it's tokenized at the source by our payment partners.
What happens to my data if I leave? +
You own your data, full stop. Export everything anytime as CSV. We'll even help you migrate to a different platform if it comes to that — but in 12+ years, that's almost never happened.
Can I see the platform before deciding? +
Absolutely. Book a 30-minute demo and we'll walk you through your exact use case — forms, payments, triggers, reporting — on a live instance. No high-pressure sales, just a working tour.
